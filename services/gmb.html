How do reviews work on Google My Business? Should I be responding to poor reviews and soliciting good ones?

Reviews play a critical part in a business’s ability to differentiate itself from the competition and can impact rankings. And yes, your business should be monitoring and replying to reviews—both good ones and bad ones.

Reviews matter! The more glowing reviews on your GMB listing, the more calls your business will get. And the more you respond to reviews, the more calls you will get. Here are five smart ways to manage reviews:

  • ✤ Respond to all reviews promptly as it encourages others to leave reviews
  • ✤ Respond to poor reviews and complaints honestly and diplomatically
  • ✤ Respect reviews as important content on your GMB page
  • ✤ Don’t obsess over 5-star reviews as customers want to see recent reviews
  • ✤ Ask past customers for reviews with specific suggestions and questions
