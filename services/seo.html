Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How does a website get to page 1 on a Google Search?

A. Google's computer algorithms decide whether content on the internet is relevant to a particular key word or topic. It then lists the websites it finds in order of their relevance (the resulting list is referred to as a Search Engine Results Page or SERP.) When someone searches for a service you provide, Google will list all the websites offering that service, in order of how relevant their content is to the topic. That’s why it is so important to have professional SEO research done ahead of time, to ensure your content is recognized as quality information by Google. Are you wondering why your website isn’t on page one? Contact us for a free SEO audit and we can tell you.

Q2. What is a conversion rate, and why is it important?

A. Conversion rate refers to the rate at which customers take action based on your website. For example, they may visit and read information, then take the action of filling out a form or making a purchase. Conversion rates are critical, because they reflect actual sales and interactions with clients, which tells you whether or not your marketing efforts are truly paying off. Don’t let your website visitors read and move on - inspire them to stop and engage with you. Once you have their attention, teach them the value your brand provides. Watch your reputation grow, one customer at a time.

Q3. What makes online content valuable to potential customers?

A. Online content is a great way for customers to educate themselves on various products and services they might require. If you provide helpful, thorough information about what you do, customers will know they can trust you as an expert in the field. Explaining the advantages of certain products or services can also help you make sales and encourage brand loyalty.

Q4. How do you track the success of your website?

A. We do a monthly audit and track the number of clicks and impressions your website gets. We then prepare a report to let you know how you are doing.

Q5. What is SVO, and how can it boost your business?

A. Search visitor optimization (SVO) refers to optimization that aims to keep website visitors on the page, reading your content. This goes beyond SEO, which simply aims to make pages rank higher on the search results page. SVO attempts to boost conversions by getting customers to read more, stay longer and engage with the company before leaving.
